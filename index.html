Frequently Asked Questions

Can I choose the dentist on my own for treatment?
Yes, you can request a specific dentist when booking your appointment. We'll do our best to accommodate your preference based on availability.
Will I get the same doctor each time I visit the same clinic?
We strive to maintain continuity of care. If you prefer to see the same dentist for follow-up visits, please mention this when scheduling your appointments.

You can book an appointment through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer service helpline. We also accept walk-ins based on availability.
